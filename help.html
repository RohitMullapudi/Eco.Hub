Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add an event?
A: You can add an event by navigating to the "Add Event" page and filling out the form. For assistance, reach out to our support team at ecohub@gmail.com.

Q: How do I find groups related to environmental topics?
A: Visit the "Find Groups" page where you can search and explore eco-friendly groups in your area.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password.
